# The Threshold Shift License (TSL)
**Version 1.0.0-draft**

This license defines a dynamic legal and economic state for digital assets (such as `.q42` ledgers, RDF ontologies, and analytic models). It explicitly rejects the infinite rent-seeking model of legacy software licensing in favor of a mathematically verifiable "Obligation Cost" threshold.

## 1. The Obligation Algorithm
The Creator of the covered work has utilized N3Logic to cryptographically bind a specific economic Obligation Cost to this asset.

The Obligation Cost is calculated using the **Risk-Compounded Obligation Algorithm**:
`Total_Obligation = (Base_Fair_Value_Rate) × (Risk_Multiplier) × (Temporal_Compound)`

- **Base_Fair_Value_Rate**: The market rate of the Creator, derived from task type, historical capability, and effort required.
- **Risk_Multiplier**: An objective scaling factor applied based on the degree to which the work was self-funded, actively objected to, or considered "crazy" by prevailing institutions. High social/financial risk yields a higher multiplier.
- **Temporal_Compound**: Time-value consideration for years of unsupported labor.
- **Fair Valuation Distribution**: The final cost is equitably distributed across all diverse resource providers who contributed to the asset.

## 2. State A: Commercial Obligation (Pre-Threshold)
Until the calculated `Total_Obligation` is met via streamed micro-payments (e.g., Interledger Protocol/Lightning):
- The Asset is strictly Commercial.
- You **may not** redistribute, copy, or host this asset without an active payment stream satisfying the WebID negotiation endpoint of the Creator.
- The Creator reserves absolute rights to the economic returns generated by this asset up to the explicit boundary of the `Total_Obligation`.

## 3. State B: Permissive Commons (Post-Threshold)
Upon cryptographic verification that accumulated network payments have equaled or exceeded the `Total_Obligation`, the N3Logic Webizen VM automatically and irreversibly shifts this license to **State B**.

In State B:
- The economic obligation has been satisfied. The Creator relinquishes further commercial rent-seeking on the core asset.
- The Asset instantly transitions into the **Permissive Commons**.
- You **may** freely copy, modify, distribute, and execute the asset.
- **Conditional Network Protection (Share-Alike)**: Any derivative works or distributions of this asset *must* actively participate in the decentralized peer network (e.g., seeding via WebTorrent DHT). You may not enclose the asset in a proprietary, centralized database without continuing to seed it to the public commons.
