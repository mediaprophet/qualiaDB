How the Compensation Model Works

1. Effort is calculated across all contributors (paid + unpaid).

2. Converted into an Obligation Cost using the current multiplier (social + outcome triggers).

3. Fulfillment paths: targeted micropayments, open donations, sponsorship, or grants.

4. Once obligation is met, assets can transition toward more permissive commons terms (per project governance).

Dynamic Equity — Slicing Pie Model Experimental
Contributions (time, money, infrastructure, AI agents) create slices at fair market value. Unpaid or high-risk contributions receive risk multipliers. Slices form a Grunt Fund that can be bought out or converted when obligations are met. Governance rights can optionally be weighted by slice ownership.
Example: Unpaid time slices can convert to commons licensing rights or revenue participation once the project’s obligation cost is fully met.
