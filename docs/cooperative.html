How Obligation & Fulfillment Works
1. Effort Calculation
Total effort across all contributors is calculated and converted into an Obligation Cost. Independent natural persons are recognized fairly alongside any cash contributions.
2. Multiple Fulfillment Paths
Obligations can be met via targeted micropayments to contributors, open donations, or sponsorship (brands pay down obligation for acknowledgement).
3. Tax & Economic Clarity
All contributions and value flows carry provenance. Designed for independent natural legal persons with clear paths for tax reporting and recognition of effort.
