Cooperative Projects

Contribute, Donate & Sponsor

Support independent contributors and help fulfill obligation costs through multiple paths.

Individual or Small Sponsor
Make a targeted contribution or sponsor specific tasks / people.
Sponsor a Task
Pay down obligation cost for a specific task. Public acknowledgement optional.
General Donation
Goes into project payment pools and is distributed fairly according to the obligation model.
Company or Institutional Sponsor
Sponsor entire phases, provide grants, or underwrite obligation costs for public good / brand alignment.
→ Phase-level sponsorship with public recognition
→ Direct support for independent contributors
→ Custom reporting on impact and obligation fulfillment
→ Potential for co-branded deliverables once obligations are met
All contributions are tracked with provenance. Sponsorship can pay down obligation costs for unpaid contributors. Terms are governed by the project’s Compensation Policy and visible in the Economics view.
